Phoenix is not just a simple plugin, it's a whole suite that can fulfill all your server's needs. It comes with 8 unique addons all for FREE that are designed to make Phoenix better, replace other plugins, and add more features to the plugin. These addons include pxDiscord and pxCosmetics and pxLevels, to name a few. By using Phoenix and its addons, you can save server resources by replacing other plugins with an all-in-one solution. Plus, the addon features make your server more interesting and fun to play on. To enable these be sure to go to Retention and enable them!
